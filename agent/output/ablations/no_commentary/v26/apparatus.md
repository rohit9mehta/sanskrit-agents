**J3. Although vijñānam could formally be nominative or accusative, the intransitive finite verb makes it the subject. The bhāṣya reading supplied here has -te, so the analysis follows the middle form rather than the vulgate active -ti.**

## Translation

> So long as consciousness (vijñāna) does not take its stand
in representation-only-ness (vijñaptimātratva),
so long the latent tendency (anuśaya)
of the twofold grasping (grāhadvaya) does not cease.
